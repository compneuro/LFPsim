LFPsim

Simulation scripts to compute Local Field Potentials (LFP) from cable compartmental models of neurons and networks implemented in NEURON simulation environment.

LFPsim works reliably on biophysically detailed multi-compartmental neurons with ion channels in some or all compartments.

LFPsim maintained by compneuro
